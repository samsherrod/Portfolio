Star Hunter - Bow and Arrow VR Project

In Development - Expected Release March 2023
Student Solo Virtual Reality Project
Role: Solo Developer

Star Hunter is a casual VR game where you can shoot stars with a bow and arrow to connect them like in Connect The Dots. When all of the stars are connected, an image will be shown, and the next set of stars will be displayed in the sky. It is currently in development built soley by myself. I'm very interested in the field of virtual reality, and have thoroughly enjoyed working on this project. It is made in the Unity game engine using Unity's XR Toolkit, and is tested for the Meta Quest 2.

This is a solo project made for University of London's Virtual Reality class. The game will be finished and playable on Windows, Mac, WebGL, and the Meta Quest 2 in March 2023.

Features of the game include:

  • a working virtual bow and arrow with precise aiming
  • virtual hands that move and animate with the player's hands
  • bow is attached to the front of the body, and the arrows are in a quiver attached to the back of the player
  • customizable menu options including the ability to toggle moving with the joystick or teleport, adjusting the height and size of the player's virtual body
  • a virtual menu
  • unlimited arrows
  • a large open area of grass to move around in
  • a nightime sky
